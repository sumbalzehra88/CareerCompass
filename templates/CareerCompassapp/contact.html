FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about CareerCompass

How do I create my first roadmap?

Simply navigate to the Dashboard and click on "Create New Roadmap". Fill in your career goals, current skills, and desired timeline. Our platform will generate a personalized career path for you.

Is CareerCompass free to use?

We offer both free and premium plans. The free plan includes basic roadmap creation and tracking. Premium features include advanced analytics, one-on-one mentorship, and exclusive resources.

Can I update my roadmap as I progress?

Absolutely! Your career journey is dynamic, and so are our roadmaps. You can update, modify, or create new roadmaps anytime as your goals and circumstances evolve.

How do I find internships and hackathons?

Browse our Internships and Hackathons pages where we curate opportunities from top companies. You can filter by location, industry, skill level, and dates to find the perfect match.

Do you offer career counseling?

Yes! Premium members get access to one-on-one sessions with experienced career counselors who can provide personalized guidance and answer specific questions about your career path.

How secure is my data?

We take data security seriously. All your information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent.
